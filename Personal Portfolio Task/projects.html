Projects

  1. Rescue Roam - Indoor Toxic Gas Detection And Human Monitoring

    August 2023 - Present

    This is my final year project that I am currently working on. The project aims to develop a cutting-edge robot for navigating toxic indoor environments, combining remote control with obstacle avoidance. Equipped with gas sensors and unobtrusive human detection, it acts as a communication relay and monitors human vital signs.

  2. Food Quality and Quantity Measurement device using IoT

    February 2023 - June 2023

    The proposed device integrates Arduino and ESP8266, along with sensors like MQ4 and LM35, to accurately measure key parameters for food quality, while also incorporating load cells to precisely measure the weight of food items for quantity assessments.
